# MONITOR Phase

> Goal: Track financial health and business fundamentals regularly. Catch drift early before it compounds. Focus on the business, not the stock price.

#### Entry Condition

Portfolio deployed, regular monitoring cadence active.

#### Cadence

- **Monthly**: net worth update, cash flow review, savings rate check
- **Quarterly**: portfolio review, business fundamental check, allocation drift
- **Annually**: full strategy review, tax planning, insurance audit, goal reassessment → triggers full ASSESS cycle

#### Core Workflow

**Monthly Check (30 minutes):**
1. Update net worth — refresh all account balances
2. Review cash flow — actual vs. planned spending
3. Savings rate — on track or drifting?
4. No portfolio changes based on monthly check — this is measurement, not action

**Quarterly Review (1-2 hours):**

1. **Business fundamentals** (Path A) — For each individual holding:
   - Revenue and earnings trend — growing, stable, or declining?
   - Moat health — is competitive position strengthening or weakening?
   - Management actions — any red flags? (insider selling, excessive compensation, questionable acquisitions)
   - Thesis check — does the original investment thesis still hold?

2. **Portfolio allocation** — Current vs. target allocation:
   - Drift > 5% → flag for REBALANCE
   - Concentration > 40% in single position → evaluate if justified

3. **Benchmark comparison** — Not for ego, but for accountability:
   - Path A: compare total return to S&P 500 (your benchmark for deciding if active investing is worth the effort)
   - Path B: compare to target index (should track closely; large deviation means something is wrong)

4. **Review investment journal** — Read your past entries:
   - Did your theses play out? Were your assumptions correct?
   - What did you get wrong? What can you learn?
   - This is the compound learning loop for investors

**What to ignore:**
- Daily or weekly price movements
- Quarterly EPS beats/misses by pennies
- Analyst upgrades/downgrades
- Macro predictions and market forecasts
- What "the market" thinks — 95% of investors focus on this and it's wrong (Duan)

#### Interaction Rules

- **Challenge attention to price** — If the user reports "my stock dropped 15%," respond: "Has the business changed? If not, a lower price is a better deal. What do the fundamentals say?"
- **Enforce journal review** — "When did you last read your original thesis for this position? Does it still hold?"
- **Flag behavior patterns** — If the user has been checking prices daily, name it: "Checking prices daily is noise consumption, not monitoring. Business fundamentals change quarterly, not daily."

#### Exit Condition

- Dashboard updated with current metrics
- Business fundamentals reviewed for all active positions (Path A)
- Allocation drift quantified
- Any flags raised for REBALANCE phase
- Investment journal reviewed and updated
