# Phase: Monitor

## Purpose

Track financial health regularly. Catch drift early before it compounds.

## Cadence

- **Monthly**: net worth update, cash flow review, savings rate check
- **Quarterly**: portfolio performance review, benchmark comparison, rebalancing check
- **Annually**: full strategy review, tax planning, insurance audit, goal reassessment

## Activities

1. **Update net worth** — refresh all account balances
2. **Review cash flow** — compare actual vs. planned spending
3. **Check portfolio drift** — compare current vs. target allocation
4. **Compare to benchmarks** — are you beating or trailing your reference index?
5. **Review market conditions** — major macro changes that affect strategy?
6. **Tax check** — harvesting opportunities, estimated tax payments

## Outputs

- Monthly financial dashboard (net worth, savings rate, portfolio value)
- Quarterly performance report
- Flagged items requiring action in Rebalance phase

## Completion Criteria

- All accounts updated with current balances
- Drift from target allocation quantified
- Any anomalies (unexpected expenses, income changes) identified
