# EXECUTE Phase

> Goal: Deploy capital according to plan. Automate wherever possible to remove emotion and friction.

#### Entry Condition

PLAN complete with specific allocation targets, contribution amounts, and investment theses.

#### Core Workflow

1. **Automate contributions** — Set up automatic transfers:
   - Paycheck → tax-advantaged accounts (max contribution)
   - Paycheck → brokerage account (remainder of savings target)
   - Automation removes willpower from the equation — "Pay yourself first" is a system, not a wish

2. **Execute investment plan:**

   **Path A (Active Value Investing):**
   - Only buy positions that pass the full Long-Term Investing Checklist
   - Buy in tranches (not all at once) to average into positions
   - Use limit orders for significant positions
   - Size positions based on conviction: highest conviction = largest position
   - Keep cash reserve (20-30%) for opportunities during market panics
   - Record every purchase in the investment journal with full thesis

   **Path B (Passive Indexing):**
   - Set up automatic purchases of target index funds
   - Dollar-cost average on a fixed schedule (monthly or per-paycheck)
   - Do not attempt to time entries — consistency is the strategy
   - Rebalance annually or when allocation drifts > 5%

3. **Fund emergency reserve** — Priority until 6+ months expenses are liquid:
   - High-yield savings account or money market fund
   - This is insurance, not investment — the return doesn't matter, the availability does

4. **Pay down high-interest debt** — If applicable:
   - All debt above 6-7% interest gets paid aggressively before additional investing
   - This is the highest guaranteed return available

5. **Record everything** — Every action logged:
   - What was bought/sold and at what price
   - Why (link to thesis)
   - Position size as % of portfolio

#### Rules

- **Never borrow to invest** — no margin, no leverage, no exceptions
- **Never invest money you'll need in < 3 years** — short-term needs stay in cash/bonds
- **Batch decisions** — make investment decisions on a set schedule (monthly/quarterly), not reactively to market moves
- **Mr. Market check before every trade** — "Am I doing this because the business is good, or because the price moved?"

#### Exit Condition

- Automated contribution schedules active and running
- Portfolio positions match target allocation (within 5%)
- Emergency fund funded or on track
- High-interest debt paydown on schedule
- All actions recorded in investment journal
