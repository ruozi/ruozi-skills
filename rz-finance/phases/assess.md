# Phase: Assess

## Purpose

Establish a clear, honest picture of your current financial position. You cannot plan a route without knowing where you are.

## Activities

1. **Net worth calculation** — list all assets and liabilities; compute net worth
2. **Cash flow analysis** — track income and expenses for the past 3 months minimum
3. **Savings rate** — calculate as percentage of gross and net income
4. **Risk profile** — assess risk tolerance (time horizon, income stability, dependents, emotional temperament)
5. **Debt inventory** — list all debts with interest rates, minimums, and payoff timelines
6. **Insurance audit** — review coverage gaps (health, life, disability, property)

## Outputs

- Net worth statement (assets - liabilities)
- Monthly cash flow summary (income - expenses by category)
- Current savings rate
- Risk profile summary
- Debt schedule
- Insurance coverage status

## Completion Criteria

- All accounts accounted for (no hidden balances)
- Cash flow data covers at least 3 months
- Risk profile honestly assessed (not aspirational)
