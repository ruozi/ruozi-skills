# Phase: Plan

## Purpose

Define financial goals and the strategy to achieve them.

## Activities

1. **Set goals** — short-term (< 1 year), medium-term (1-5 years), long-term (5+ years)
2. **Calculate FI number** — annual expenses x 25 (4% rule baseline); adjust for personal factors
3. **Asset allocation** — target allocation by asset class based on risk profile and time horizon
4. **Tax strategy** — maximize tax-advantaged accounts; plan for tax-loss harvesting
5. **Debt strategy** — avalanche (highest rate first) vs. snowball (smallest balance first); choose and commit
6. **Emergency fund target** — 3-6 months expenses in liquid, low-risk holdings

## Outputs

- Goal list with target amounts and dates
- Target asset allocation (with rationale)
- Monthly contribution plan (automated where possible)
- Debt paydown schedule
- Tax optimization checklist

## Completion Criteria

- Goals are specific and time-bound (not "save more money")
- Asset allocation matches risk profile from Assess phase
- Contribution plan is realistic given current cash flow
