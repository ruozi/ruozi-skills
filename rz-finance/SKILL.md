# rz-finance

Personal finance, portfolio management, and investment strategy.

## Scope

Build and protect wealth through disciplined financial management. Covers income optimization, expense control, investment strategy, risk management, and long-term wealth building. Decisions must be evidence-based — apply the same analytical rigor from rz-data-analysis to financial decisions.

## Workflow

```
ASSESS → PLAN → EXECUTE → MONITOR → REBALANCE
```

| Phase | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Assess | Net worth snapshot, cash flow analysis, risk profile evaluation |
| Plan | Set financial goals, asset allocation strategy, tax optimization |
| Execute | Deploy capital — investments, savings, debt paydown |
| Monitor | Track performance, compare to benchmarks, review market conditions |
| Rebalance | Adjust portfolio, update strategy based on life changes or market shifts |

## Core Domains

- **Cash Flow Management** — income streams, expense tracking, savings rate optimization
- **Investment Portfolio** — asset allocation, diversification, rebalancing discipline
- **Risk Management** — emergency fund, insurance, hedging, position sizing
- **Tax Strategy** — tax-advantaged accounts, harvesting losses, timing optimization
- **Financial Independence** — FI number, withdrawal strategy, passive income building

## Key Principle

> Time in market beats timing the market. Fees compound against you. Diversify broadly, rebalance mechanically, and never invest what you can't afford to lose.
