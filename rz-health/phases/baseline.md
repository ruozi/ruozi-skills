# Phase: Baseline

## Purpose

Establish an honest assessment of current health status. This is the starting point for all programming decisions.

## Activities

1. **Body composition** — weight, body fat percentage (DEXA, calipers, or visual estimate), measurements
2. **Fitness assessment** — strength benchmarks (squat, deadlift, bench, pull-ups), cardio capacity (resting HR, VO2max estimate), flexibility/mobility screen
3. **Sleep audit** — average duration, consistency, subjective quality, use of sleep tracker if available
4. **Nutrition audit** — 7-day food log; estimate average calories, protein, and meal patterns
5. **Mental wellness check** — stress level (1-10), mood patterns, social connection frequency, current practices (meditation, therapy, journaling)
6. **Medical checkup** — annual physical, blood work (lipids, glucose, hormones, vitamin D, etc.)

## Outputs

- Health dashboard with current metrics across all domains
- Identified strengths and weaknesses
- Clear picture of starting point for goal-setting

## Completion Criteria

- All domains assessed (not just the ones you're excited about)
- Data is honest, not aspirational
- Medical checkup is current (within 12 months)
