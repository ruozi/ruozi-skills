# Phase: Train

## Purpose

Execute the program. Show up consistently and do the work.

## Activities

1. **Follow the program** — do what's planned, resist the urge to freelance
2. **Log workouts** — sets, reps, weights, RPE (rate of perceived exertion)
3. **Follow nutrition plan** — hit calorie and protein targets; don't stress minor deviations
4. **Prioritize sleep** — protect bedtime; this is where gains are made
5. **Practice mental wellness** — meditation, journaling, or whatever protocol is in the program
6. **Listen to your body** — distinguish between discomfort (push through) and pain (back off)

## Key Rules

- **Never skip twice** — missing one session is human; missing two is a pattern
- **Minimum effective dose** — on low-energy days, a reduced workout beats no workout
- **Form over ego** — reduce weight before compromising technique
- **Hydrate** — drink water consistently throughout the day

## Completion Criteria

- Adherence rate > 80% over the training block
- All sessions logged
- No training through actual pain/injury
