# Phase: Track

## Purpose

Monitor key metrics to detect trends, celebrate progress, and flag issues early.

## Metrics

| Domain | Metrics | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Body | Weight, body fat, measurements | Weekly |
| Strength | Key lift numbers, rep PRs | Per session |
| Cardio | Resting HR, run times, VO2max | Monthly |
| Sleep | Duration, consistency, quality score | Daily |
| Nutrition | Average calories, protein, adherence | Weekly |
| Mental | Stress level, mood, energy | Weekly |

## Activities

1. **Weekly check-in** — weight trend, training adherence, energy/mood assessment
2. **Monthly review** — fitness benchmarks, body composition changes, sleep averages
3. **Quarterly assessment** — full baseline re-test to measure progress against goals

## Outputs

- Weekly health dashboard update
- Monthly progress summary
- Quarterly progress report with goal comparison

## Completion Criteria

- Tracking is consistent (no multi-week gaps)
- Trends are identified (not just point-in-time snapshots)
- Flags raised for any metric trending in wrong direction for 2+ weeks
