# Phase: Program

## Purpose

Design a structured plan for training, nutrition, sleep, and recovery based on goals and baseline.

## Activities

1. **Set goals** — specific, measurable, time-bound (e.g., "squat 1.5x bodyweight by Q4", "sleep 7.5+ hours 6 nights/week")
2. **Training program** — exercise selection, frequency, volume, progression scheme
3. **Nutrition plan** — calorie target, macro split, meal structure (not obsessive meal prep — sustainable patterns)
4. **Sleep protocol** — bedtime/wake time targets, wind-down routine, environment optimization
5. **Recovery plan** — rest days, deload schedule, mobility/stretching routine
6. **Mental wellness plan** — meditation schedule, journaling cadence, social commitments

## Outputs

- Weekly training schedule
- Daily nutrition targets
- Sleep and recovery protocols
- Defined check-in cadence (weekly weigh-in, monthly fitness test, quarterly review)

## Completion Criteria

- Program is realistic given current schedule and commitments
- Progression is built in (not just "do the same thing forever")
- Recovery is explicitly programmed, not an afterthought
